About Me

My name is Puseletso Mphamo, an Information Technology graduate with a strong passion for innovation, problem solving, and building meaningful digital solutions. I hold an IIE Diploma in IT Network Management and continue to grow my knowledge in the technology field.

Throughout my academic journey I have gained valuable knowledge in networking, cybersecurity, artificial intelligence fundamentals, and software development. I successfully completed the Cisco Networking Academy Introduction to Cyber Security certification and the Microsoft Introduction to Artificial Intelligence certification, strengthening my understanding of emerging technologies.

I am currently enrolled in the Java Skills Programme at Mecer Inter-Ed, where I am developing my skills in Java programming, problem solving, and modern software development practices.

My journey reflects the growing influence of women in technology. Through continuous learning and determination, I aim to contribute to innovative solutions while inspiring others to pursue careers in the IT industry.
